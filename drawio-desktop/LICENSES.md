Trellis for Drawio does not relicense upstream draw.io code, draw.io trademarks, or third-party material.

## Trellis-Owned Additions

Trellis-owned additions include the garden planner plugins, Trellis database seed/schema content, Trellis bridge changes, Trellis release/update UI, and Trellis documentation authored for this project.

This source-available permission is not an OSI open source license because commercial use is restricted.

## Commercial Use

Commercial use of Trellis-owned additions requires written approval from Benjamin Elon before use. Payment or Patreon support alone does not grant commercial rights unless it is tied to written approval.

Approved commercial organizations must maintain the approved Patreon support level or other written payment arrangement for commercial use. If support lapses because of a missed payment, failed card, or similar issue, the organization has a 30-day cure period after notice to restore support or obtain updated written permission.

Commercial approval may include organization-specific financial, social, attribution, collaboration, or contribution terms.

## Modified Trellis Code

Private internal modifications do not have to be shared back.

If an approved commercial organization distributes modified Trellis-owned code, plugins, or source-available Trellis files to clients, users, customers, or downstream products, those distributed modifications must be shared back with the project under terms that let Trellis continue to publish and commercially license them.

## Contributions

External code contributions are not accepted until the contributor has granted the project the rights needed to publish, modify, redistribute, and commercially license the contribution. A contributor license agreement or equivalent written permission is required before accepting external code.

Ideas, issue reports, testing notes, and non-code feedback are welcome through the project contact channels.

## Contact

Commercial licensing, collaboration, support, and contribution questions should use the public contact links shown in the Trellis for Drawio startup dialog and Updates & Links dialog. Placeholder contact links may appear until the public channels are finalized.
